Level 2 High-Powered Rocket: Detailed Overview

Context

This rocket is hybrid between the L1 I launched and the L2 I plan to launch in April. I wanted to gain more experience with desinging, building, and launching rockets before going for my L2 certification.

Content

I am designing Red-Winged Blackbird to familiarize myself with working on avionics and composites. It will be flying on the I435T at LUNAR. I am using a pre-assembled egg timer AV board with dual-deploy parachute capability and have 3D printed my AV sled and endcaps. I also learned how to integrate an AV into my rocket. I learned how to make my own nose cone with fiberglass sleeves and epoxy, including how to safely cut and sand the fiber glass. Additionally, I CADed and printed the shoulder of my nose cone which now slides into my upper airframe.
