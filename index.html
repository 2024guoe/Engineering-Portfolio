Engineering Portfolio

Hello, I'm Ellen! I am a freshman at Stanford University, majoring in Mechanical Engineering. I am passionate about problem-solving and pushing the boundaries of innovation.


At Stanford, I'm pursuing my interests in several projects. On the Student Space Initiative (SSI), I work on Propulsion within the Rocketry team, engineering injectors that bridge the gap between our liquid and solid propellants for rocket Fountain Hopper. Since joining SSI, I have also attained my L1 High-Power Rocketry certificate and am currently designing my rocket for my L2 certificate. As an amateur ornithologist, I am working on a personal project involving hummingbirds sponsored by Moonshot Club. I'm also an Academic Development intern for the Society of Undergraduate Mechanical Engineers (SUME), Stanford's branch of ASME, where I plan and execute academic and research related events for MechE undergrads.


When I'm not tinkering away on my variety of projects, I enjoy playing card games, swimming, playing viola with my string trio, bird-watching while hiking, and painting.
